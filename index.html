The 60 / 30 / 10 view

Most of what a boutique agency ships falls into three layers. Only one of them is really AI.

60%

Traditional software

Client intake, brief assembly, approval routing, asset handling. This layer is code and databases doing what they do well.

30%

Rule-based logic

Brand guardrails. Bilingual register rules. Approval thresholds. Known-answer work that belongs in rules you can read.

10%

Genuine AI

The drafting, the summarizing, the voice work. Magic lands here, but only if the first two layers are solid.

Applied to Minnie Marketing Co.: the bilingual voice for each client lives in a locked context folder. The intake template lives in code. AI drafts land in the client's voice on the first pass instead of the fifth. That is the whole shift.
